AgriConnectMG Site Plan

Site Name

AgriConnectMG

The name "AgriConnectMG" represents the main goal of the website: connecting farmers and buyers in Madagascar. "Agri" refers to agriculture, "Connect" signifies the connection between farmers and consumers, and "MG" stands for Madagascar.

Site Purpose

This website serves as a digital marketplace for agricultural products in Madagascar. It allows farmers to list and sell their products directly to buyers, eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair pricing. Buyers can easily find fresh and affordable products.

Scenarios

Visitors to the site might ask:

  • Where can I find fresh fruits and vegetables near me?
  • How can I sell my farm products directly to consumers?

Color Scheme

The website will use a natural color palette:

  • Green (#2E7D32): Used for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • Earthy Brown (#795548): Used for secondary elements and footer.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Used for background and text readability.

Typography

The following fonts will be used:

  • Montserrat (Headings): A modern, bold font for titles.
  • Open Sans (Body Text): A readable, professional font for content.
